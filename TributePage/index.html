TWICE

The female group that revolutionized the K-pop industry

Biography

Twice is a popular South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015 through the reality show "Sixteen." The group consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. They quickly gained recognition for their catchy songs, impressive choreography, and charming personalities. Twice has become one of the best-selling K-pop groups of all time, known for hits like "Cheer Up," "TT," and "Fancy." They have won numerous awards and gained a massive international fanbase, solidifying their status as one of the leading acts in the global K-pop phenomenon.
